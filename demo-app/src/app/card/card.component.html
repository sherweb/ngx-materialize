Colors and hoverable

Optionally, colors can be customized using backgroundClass and/or textClass input properties but are not required.

An animation for box-shadow can also be added using hoverable input properties. Roll your cursor over and out of the card to see the animation.

HTML Structure
<mz-card [backgroundClass]="'blue-grey darken-1'" [textClass]="'white-text'" [hoverable]="true"> <mz-card-title> Card Title </mz-card-title> <mz-card-content> I am a very simple card. I am good at containing small bits of information. I am convenient because I require little markup to use effectively. </mz-card-content> <mz-card-action> <a href="#">This is a link</a> <a href="#">This is a link</a> </mz-card-action> </mz-card>
